why I read

I like to read to train my eyes. Everyday I feel like my pupils are becoming weaker. I guess I also like to train my brain too.

But more than that, I like to collect things. And every book I finish I see as an opportunity to buy a copy of and add to my collection.

I like to read new books on my kindle first, then if I like it a lot, I consider buying a physical copy to refer to, lend, and reread.
